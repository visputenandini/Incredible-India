Karnataka

About Karnataka

Karnataka is located in the southern part of India. It is famous for its rich heritage, historical temples, IT industry in Bengaluru, classical music, and traditional silk.

Famous Food

Dosa, Mysore Pak, Bisi Bele Bath, and Neer Dosa are popular dishes of Karnataka.

Heritage

Hampi, Mysore Palace, and Pattadakal are UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Karnataka.

Culture

Karnataka is known for Carnatic music, Yakshagana dance, and traditional silk sarees.

Famous Place

Bengaluru, Coorg, Udupi, and Hampi are well-known travel destinations.
